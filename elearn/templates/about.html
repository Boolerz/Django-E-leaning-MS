Want to know about us ?

Bright Horizon Academy is a new online school in Kenya founded by Fondo Rashid, founder & CEO of Boolerz Limited Company.The main aim of Bright Horizon is to offering quality secondary education. We cater to students who have missed traditional schooling, especially those impacted by the phasing out of the 8-4-4 curriculum. Our focus is on providing flexible learning, qualified teachers, and comprehensive KCSE preparation.
